Books Summary Plot Analysis Character Analysis Language Analysis Review
Our community consists of a rainbow of book lovers; ranging from classical romance to historical thriller to fantasy adventure, we strive to bring genres from polar ends at one destination. Our most popular branch is the art of summarising. We sum up the entire journey, and while doing so, reflect on key ideas that are either central to the plot or are appealing to us. Then there are plot-centered book lovers, who spend a good time marvelling at the beauty, transition and churns in the plot. They spend time in reading, processing, predicting, analysing and reflecting the plot. Similarly, our character-centered book lovers, who value characterisation above everything, analyse the characters. They empathise and truly feel the surmount of emotions; and then... the story begins to sing to them. Linguistic experts put their heads together to appreciate the writer's talent- the writer's skill in putting everything into words in breathing life to it. For everyone out there, who prefer to get a glimpse of a book to motivate them to begin reading one and anyone who is picky, our book reviews will tell you everything you need to know, shrewdly leaking the enticing parts without really revealing it completely.



Our AIM is to bring together book lovers across the globe, to a common platform.
A platform to share ideas, thoughts, opinions, interpretations and insights.
Our mission is to create and encourage the art of 'reading books'.
